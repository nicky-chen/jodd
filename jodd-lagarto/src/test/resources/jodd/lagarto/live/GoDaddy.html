Domains
Domain registration is Go Daddy's primary business. An ICANN-accredited registrar, the company currently manages over 48 million domain names for more than nine million customers worldwide. Go Daddy is the largest domain name registrar in the world and is more than four times the size of its closest competitor.

Email
In addition to domains, Go Daddy also offers fast, secure email accounts. Every GoDaddy.com email address is personalized with the owner’s domain name, making it easy for friends and business associates to remember. All email plans come with built-in protection from viruses and spam and can be used with iPhone®, Blackberry®, Droid® and other handheld devices. Businesses will be interested in Hosted Exchange email – it provides all the performance of corporate-class email without the maintenance.

Web hosting
The world's largest hosting provider of secure websites, Go Daddy offers advanced 4th-generation hosting that uses a unique system of server clusters to reduce outages and overage fees for its hosting customers. Compared to the 2nd-generation Web hosting still sold by many hosting companies, Go Daddy’s 4GH hosting offers 300% more capacity with no effect on site performance. The company also offers WordPress®, blog and podcast hosting, as well as virtual dedicated, dedicated and Mac-powered cloud servers.

Online security products
GoDaddy.com helps customers combat Internet fraud and theft with a variety of affordable security tools. These include SSL Certificates, Premium DNS, Site Scanner and a Code Signing Certificate. Go Daddy is the top provider of net new SSL certificates in the world.

Website builders
WebSite Tonight®, Go Daddy's easy sitebuilder, allows do-it-yourselfers to quickly build and launch their own website on the Internet. For customers who want to sell products or services online, there’s Quick Shopping Cart® which can be used alone or in conjunction with WebSite Tonight to create a fully functional Web store.

For those who don’t have the desire or time to build their own website, Go Daddy's Dream Design Team is available to do it for them. This in-house graphic design group creates terrific-looking websites, eCommerce sites and business logos for a very reasonable fee.

Business
There are a number of GoDaddy.com products designed to help entrepreneurs do business on the Web. In addition to Quick Shopping Cart, Go Daddy offers an easy marketing email creator, tools to improve website rankings on search engines and productivity aids like Online File Folder® and Online Calendar.

Ways to make money
Go Daddy gives customers several ways to make money – sell our products through a pre-built Web store, sell our white-labeled products on their own website or place Go Daddy pay-per-click ads on their Web pages.

About Go Daddy
Established by CEO Bob Parsons in 1997, Go Daddy has appeared on the Deloitte Technology Fast 500 list five years in a row and on Inc. magazine's Inc. 500/5000 list seven years running. Every year, the company gives millions of dollars to groups such as Hope for Haiti, Make-A-Wish, the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation, Southwest Center for HIV/AIDS, the Arizona Humane Society, the Child Crisis Center of Arizona and the Salvation Army.
